Process Paper

        The topic I chose was the Cuban Missile Crisis. I chose this topic because it sounded interesting.  I was trying to abide by the NHD theme of conflict and compromise in the world.  I thought that this topic was a perfect example of what conflict and compromise means. I felt that it would be easy to explain how this related with Conflict and Compromise because the conflict was that the US had missiles in Turkey and that the Soviets had missiles in Cuba.  The Compromise was that both countries took their missiles out of each of the allied territories. I conducted my research by first looking up a brief summary of each topic I wanted to do to see if it fit with the NHD theme of Conflict and Compromise. Once I found one that fit the criteria (The Cuban Missile Crisis), I started to do some more in-depth research.  I did most of my research on .org sites and .gov so I could ensure that all of my information was accurate and not false.  I also ran all of my work through google translate to see how it sounded.  After I had a good amount of sources and videos my next step was to pick a project category.  I chose to do a website because when I go to college I want to major in computer science and I thought that if I did a project on a website it would be a great learning experience. I've always loved computers and technology this sounded like an awesome way to try to put together my own website.  The way my project relates to the NHD theme is because it shows probably one of the greatest examples of Conflict and Compromise.  Kennedy and Khrushchev chose a peaceful resolution by talking it out and choosing to take both of their missiles out of Turkey and Cuba. Even though the world was on the brink of nuclear war we were able to avoid it peacefully.
